Frequently Asked Questions

Is the resume builder really free?

Yes, absolutely! You can create, edit, and download as many resumes as you like without any cost.

Do I need to create an account?

You can fill the form without logging in, but you’ll need to log in to download the PDF and save your resumes.

Can I upload my own photo?

Of course! The form includes a photo upload field (max 100KB, JPG/PNG) so your resume looks complete.

Will my resume be ATS‑friendly?

Yes, all templates are designed with clean layouts, semantic structure, and simple fonts to pass ATS scans.
